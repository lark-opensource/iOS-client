Unable to connect to the internet

If you cannot connect to the internet, try the following methods:

1. If you are using iOS 10 or above, and it does not allow 'Feishu' to use WLAN and Cellular mobile networks:

Go to Settings - Cellular Data - Apps Using WLAN & Cellular and allow 'Feishu' to use data on your iPhone.

If you can't find "Feishu" in Apps Using WLAN & Cellular, try switching the permissions of other apps before finding 'Feishu' to allow the use of data.

2. If WLAN or Cellular Network is not enabled on your iPhone

To connect to the internet, try:

Go to Settings - WLAN, and connect to a Wi-Fi hotspot, or, go to Settings - Cellular, and enable Cellular Data.

3. If you're already connected to a Wi-Fi network

- Please check whether the Wi-Fi hotspot you are using to is connected to the Internet, or whether the hotspot allows your iPhone to access the Internet.
